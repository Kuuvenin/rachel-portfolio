Overview

The Necronomichive is a fictional archive dedicated to cults, forbidden rituals, and esoteric traditions inspired by the works of H. P. Lovecraft. Designed as a digital repository of hidden knowledge, the project combines archival aesthetics with occult themes to create an immersive browsing experience.
